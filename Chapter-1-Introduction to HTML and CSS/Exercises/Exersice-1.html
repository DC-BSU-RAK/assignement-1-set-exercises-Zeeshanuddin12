Creme Brulee Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of heavy cream (that's about 500 ml)
  • 1 vanilla bean (or just use 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract if that's easier)
  • 5 large egg yolks
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar (plus a little extra to sprinkle on top later)
  • A pinch of salt

Procedure

  1. Preheat Oven

    2. Preheat the oven to 150°C (300°F).Heat gently over heat until warm (not boiling).Cool and leave aside for 10-15 minutes. Remove vanilla pod.

  2. Prepare Egg Mix

    3. Beat together the egg yolks, sugar, and pinches of salt in a bowl until pale yellow.

  3. Temper the Eggs

    4. Beat together the egg yolks, sugar, and pinches of salt in a bowl until pale yellow.

  4. Strain and Pour

    5. Strain into a jug to avoid lumps.Fill equal amounts into the ramekins.

  5. Bake in a Water Bath

    6. Half fill the tray with hot water, to the ramekin sides. Bake for 30–35 minutes until the custard is set (the centre will still jiggle slightly).

  6. Caramelize the Top

    7. Dust each chilled custard with 1 teaspoon of sugar. Caramelize the sugar with a kitchen torch until golden and crunchy. (If you don't have a torch, you can place the ramekins under a broiler on high for 2–3 minutes, keeping an eye on them.)
